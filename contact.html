Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about dining at Savory Bites

We welcome both walk-ins and reservations, though reservations are highly recommended, especially on weekends and during peak dining hours. You can book a table through our website, by phone, or using the reservation form.

We maintain a business casual dress code. While we want you to be comfortable, we ask that guests avoid wearing beachwear, athletic wear, or flip-flops. Jackets are preferred but not required for gentlemen.

Yes, we're happy to accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies. Please inform your server about any dietary needs when you arrive, or note them in your reservation. Our chefs can modify many dishes to suit vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements.

We offer valet parking for $15, or there are several public parking garages within a 2-block radius. Street parking is also available but can be limited during peak hours.

Absolutely! We have private dining rooms available for groups from 10 to 50 guests. Our events team can create customized menus for your special occasion. Please contact our events coordinator at events@savorybites.com or call (555) 123-4567 ext. 2 for more information.
